What is Next step?

Next step is a UCN's program that is designed to help ideas and start-up businesses to flourish and grow. I have joined Next step programme with my former classmate and together we worked on the project called Spots (see Projects - Spots). The internship program offered methoring in various areas (marketing, finances, etc.) and different meetings and activities. During the week we would have various methoring sessions and in the end of the week every one would present the progress of the company in front of each other. Additionally there were many pitching events with investors. Finally, in May, we participated on a start-up contest in Aarhus.
