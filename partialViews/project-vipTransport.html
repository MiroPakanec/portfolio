VIP Transport

March 2016 - October 2016
A Web Application developed that helps to manage transportations

What is VIP Transport

VIP Transport is a Slovak company that offers high quality transportations of personel. The web application was designed to help VIP Transport to solve 3 different problems:

● improve customer experience - make it easier for customers to create, view and manage their orders and transportations and collect their feedback and reviews.

● improve internal communication - help company managers and employees to effectivelly communicate and track each others activities

● collect data and provide statistics - the application tracks all orders, customers and payments and therefore provides a useful company overview for the managers

Technologies behind VIP Transport

The web application was developed using the XAMPP stack with addition to NoSQL mongoDB for statistics and data analisys.
