About

Software development is a 2 years long Top-up program at UCN and it is a continuation to Computer Science AP Degree program. In comparison with Computer Science, it is much more theoretical. It has a greater focus on Math, for example a Distrite mathematics course in the 1st semester. Other courses that I partecipated on are:

● Test - learning about test plans, strategies, approaches, testable architecture, types of tests and their implementation
● Databases - a more advanced course on relational databases, their design, creation, optimization and security.
