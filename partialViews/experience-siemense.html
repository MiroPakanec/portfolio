What project was I working on?

When you are reading and reporting, the last thing you want to think about is navigating through a cumbersome visualization software. You want to focus on your case, your diagnosis, your findings, your patient. syngo.via1 is the intelligent imaging software for multi-modality reading that helps you master growing amounts of imaging data in less time. Work with a clear screen that is tailored to your style of reading, with less clicks and more ease. In short: syngo.via is reading as it should be.
