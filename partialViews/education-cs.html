About

Computer Science is a 5 semester long AP Degree program at UCN. I joined the class in the 2nd semester. Luckily I have finished the semester with a 12 and I managed to hold this standard untill the end (well almost - 11.67). What I really like about this education is that we almost always worked in groups and therefore I learned a lot about how to work in a team as well as how to develop software in a team (Agile, SCRUM, XP).
